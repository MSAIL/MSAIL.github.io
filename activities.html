Activities

Reading Group

Tuesdays at 18:00 in BBB 3725

Before each week's meeting, members read literature related to the week's topic. At the meeting, a volunteer leader briefly presents, then guides discussion. See Slack for more information!

Projects

Tuesdays and Sundays

MSAIL engages in exploratory coding projects. We hold group coding-brainstorming-(progress-updating) sessions weekly on Sundays. We also hold office hours on Tuesdays at 19:00 in BBB 3725 and at other times by appointment to ensure project team members have the tools and direction they need. See Slack for more information!

Tutorials

Coordinates Vary

MSAIL occasionally offers tutorials. Our most recent tutorial teaches TensorFlow basics. See Slack for more information!
