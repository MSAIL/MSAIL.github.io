FAQ
I’m not in the College of Engineering, can I still join?
YES! All that we ask of our members is an interest in Machine Learning and a willingness to learn. As long as you meet these criteria, you’re all set to join, regardless of major or school.
I’ve not taken any classes on Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning, can I still join?
YES! We don’t assume any formal training in Machine Learning or AI, in fact many of our members had not taken EECS 445, 492, or 442 before joining MSAIL. We encourage you to check out one of our classes as part of our weekly curriculum.
I’ve not taken any coding classes, can I still join?
YES! Although be forewarned, some of what we cover in our groups might be a little over your head. That being said, we have a weekly seminary series targeted at students with little to no familiarity with the tools of Machine Learning and Data Science.
It’s the middle of the semester, most student orgs have closed recruiting; can I still join MSAIL?
YES! You’re in luck, MSAIL takes new members on a rolling basis, which means you can apply to join now if you would like. Be aware that some of our groups will have already started work, and may not be taking new members, but there’s still plenty of ways to get involved. IF you are interested in joing MSAIL, contact Aaron Pollack.
I’m new to Machine Learning and want to learn more about the tools used, how do I do this?
Here’s a list of resources relating to Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. This should get you started. If you have any specific questions, feel free to contact us. We’ve likely had your question before, and can at least point you in the direction of something that answers it.
I saw something really awesome relating to Machine Learning, Data Science, or Artificial Intelligence, how can I share it with the group?
If you have a resource, tutorial, paper, or project you’d like to share with MSAIL's membership, contact us and we might just put it on our site for all to see.
