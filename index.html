How do I join?
First, please fill out the application. This is not meant to be a selective process, but rather an understanding of your background and what you'd like to get out of MSAIL. That way, we can recommend the best way you can engage with the MSAIL community.
To make MSAIL approachable to anyone, we've devised five activities one can participate in that range in different "experience levels". Where you fit into these are up to your discretion, but we have a few guidelines:
  • CASUAL - Interested in learning Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, but can't make a weekly commitment? We still want you to be the community! For this semester, we recommend you check out our Speaker Series. Speakers will be posted on the schedule page. If you have an idea for a talk, please email us at msail-contact@umich.edu.
  • BEGINNERS - You've taken some programming class but would like a working knowledge of Machine Learning. We recommend a regular commmitment, weekly or semi-weekly, to get the most out of this. This semester, we will have a 6-8 week Tutorial teaching the basic packages and background of Machine Learning. After this level, we hope you'll either use these new skills in our own research, internships, and classes. If you'd like to stay connected to MSAIL, we encourage you stay along for the "ADVANCED" level activities.
  • ADVANCED - This level requires more consistent commmitment. We have several offerings:
  • Project Teams
  • Data Science Team
  • Reading Group
