What is MSAIL?

MSAIL is a student organization devoted to artificial intelligence research. We strive to spread our passion for artificial intelligence throughout the University of Michigan student body, regardless of demographic or academic standing. Our main operations are our reading group discussions, our education sessions, our blog, and our projects.
